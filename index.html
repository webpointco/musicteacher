Namaste

My name is Padmanabhan S. I am a Carnatic vocal artist, Gana Praveen (7 year course) in music from Sree Swathi Thirunal College of Music at Trivandrum, India.

I am very passionate about music and have decades of experience teaching music for students at all levels. I teach individual and group classes and am flexible on the time.

I am also the temple Bhagavathar for the iconic Padmanabha Swamy temple in Trivandrum.

If anyone is interested in learning music, please contact me.
